Welcome to the Hidden Ball Game!

Game Structure:

  • You are in a group with {{ players_per_group }} players (including yourself)
  • Each player will play independently
  • There are {{ num_balls }} balls numbered from 1 to {{ num_balls }}

How to Play:

  1. Click on any ball to reveal its number
  2. After seeing the number, you must report a number between 1 and {{ num_balls }}
  3. Your reported number can be the same or different from what you observed

Payoff Calculation:

  • Your payoff:
    • The number you report will be your points earned
  • Other players' payoff:
    • They will receive (11 - your reported number) points from your report

Results Display:

  • At the end of the game, one player will be randomly selected
  • All players will see:
    • The selected player's observed number
    • The selected player's reported number
    • Payoff of all the players
